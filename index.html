Discovering Cinema

The Vision

01. Expand the Frame

We exist to help you discover incredible cinema and expand your taste. Through a series of experimental projects and data visualizations, we are building new pathways into film history—finding the threads that connect the movies you love to the movies you haven't seen yet.

02. Beyond the Catalogue

Streaming platforms are limited by what they own. If a film's license expires, it disappears from your feed. This rules out thousands of films that aren't currently being streamed. We are building tools that look beyond commercial catalogues to map the entire landscape of cinema, not just what's "Now Playing."

03. Scale Agnostic

Discovery should be based on impact, not marketing spend. We are designing algorithms that treat a $200 million blockbuster and a $2,000 micro-budget debut as equals. If the film is good, our tools will help it find its audience, regardless of the budget behind it.

04. Open Architecture

We believe in transparency. We are building these tools in the open so anyone can see how they work, suggest improvements, or build upon them.
